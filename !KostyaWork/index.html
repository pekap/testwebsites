Main formulas
Initial equation: \[ \left( \begin{array}{ccc} M_x & M_y & M_z \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{ccc} 1 & \delta & \delta \\ \delta & 1 & \delta \\ \delta & \delta & 1 \end{array} \right)^{-1} \left( \begin{array}{c} f_x \\ f_y \\ f_z \end{array} \right)< 1 \] Then we can bound central matrix: \[ \left( \begin{array}{ccc} M_x & M_y & M_z \end{array} \right) \frac{1}{1-3\delta^2-2\delta^3} \left( \begin{array}{ccc} 1+\delta^2 & \delta+\delta^2 & \delta+\delta^2 \\ \delta+\delta^2 & 1+\delta^2 & \delta+\delta^2 \\ \delta+\delta^2 & \delta+\delta^2 & 1+\delta^2 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} f_x \\ f_y \\ f_z \end{array} \right)< 1 \] Lets make some denotions: \[ \begin{array}{cc} \xi=\frac{1+\delta^2}{1-3\delta^2-2\delta^3} & \zeta = \frac{\delta+\delta^2}{1-3\delta^2-2\delta^3} \end{array} \] So: \[ \left( \begin{array}{ccc} M_x & M_y & M_z \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{ccc} \xi & \zeta & \zeta \\ \zeta & \xi & \zeta \\ \zeta & \zeta & \xi \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} f_x \\ f_y \\ f_z \end{array} \right)< 1 \] After multiplication: \[ M_x(\xi f_x + \zeta f_y + \zeta f_z)+ M_y(\xi f_y + \zeta f_x + \zeta f_z)+ M_z(\xi f_z + \zeta f_x + \zeta f_y) < 1 \] Which is true if the following is true: \[ \begin{array}{c} M_x(\xi f_x + \zeta f_y + \zeta f_z) < \frac{1}{3} \\ M_y(\xi f_y + \zeta f_x + \zeta f_z) < \frac{1}{3} \\ M_z(\xi f_z + \zeta f_x + \zeta f_y) < \frac{1}{3} \end{array} \] Hence the follwing should be true: \[ \begin{array}{c} f_y + f_z < \frac{1}{\zeta}(\frac{1}{3M_x} - f_x \xi)=K_x \\ f_x + f_z < \frac{1}{\zeta}(\frac{1}{3M_y} - f_y \xi)=K_y \\ f_x + f_y < \frac{1}{\zeta}(\frac{1}{3M_z} - f_z \xi)=K_z \end{array} \] Which is in its turn true if: \[ \begin{array}{cc} 2 f_y < K_x & 2 f_z < K_x\\ 2 f_x < K_y & 2 f_z < K_y\\ 2 f_x < K_z & 2 f_y < K_z \end{array} \] Filter our graph once again (remain only those lines \(\{i,j\}\) that satisfy \(K_i>2f_j\) \(K_j>2f_i \)) Now all cliques of size 3 in graph represent guaranteed safe lines
\( \textbf{Better way:} \) Lets fix \( (y,z)\) pair: \[ \begin{eqnarray} \xi(\frac{\zeta}{\xi}(M_y+M_z)+M_x)(\frac{\zeta}{\xi}(f_y+f_z)+f_x)-\frac{\zeta^2}{\xi}(M_y+M_z)(f_y+f_z)+ \\ +\xi (M_y f_y+M_z f_z)+\zeta (M_y f_z+M_z f_y)<1 \end{eqnarray} \]
Denoting \( \mu (y,z) = \frac{\zeta}{\xi}(M_y+M_z) \), \( \phi (y,z) = \frac{\zeta}{\xi}(f_y+f_z) \) and \( \theta (y,z) = M_y f_y + M_z f_z \) we get: \[ (\mu(y,z)+M_x)(\phi(y,z)+f_x) < \frac{1}{\xi} - \frac{\xi - \zeta }{\xi}(\theta (y,z) + \frac{\xi}{\zeta}\mu(y,z) \phi(x,y) ) \] Or: \[ (\mu(y,z)+M_x)(\phi(y,z)+f_x) < \Gamma(y,z) \] Hence for fixed pair of indexes \( (y,z) \) we should define all \( x \) that satisfy equation. We want to do it in \( O(N^{c}_{y,z}) \) operations (number of contingencies for fixed pair of indexes. It can be done with Quadtree method. Firstly we divide our plane in rectangulars. Then define rectangulars that are above or on the hyperbole for fixed pair \( (y,z) \). Finally we determine which \( x \) are lying in those rectangulars. The computational complexity of such algorithm should be \( O(log(N^{c}_{y,z})N^{c}_{y,z}) \)
